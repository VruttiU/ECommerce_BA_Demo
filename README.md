# E-Commerce Platform - Business Analysis Case Study

## Project Overview

This repository showcases a complete Business Analysis case study for an E-Commerce platform developed as a portfolio project. The project demonstrates the end-to-end Business Analysis lifecycle, including requirement gathering, documentation, Agile planning, process modeling, and UI wireframing.

The objective was to analyze business requirements for a retail company transitioning from manual sales operations to a scalable online e-commerce platform.

---

## Business Problem

A mid-sized retail business lacked a centralized digital sales platform, resulting in:

- Manual order processing
- Order fulfillment delays
- Customer dissatisfaction
- Limited payment options
- No real-time order tracking
- Poor inventory visibility

The solution was to design and document a complete Business Analysis solution for a modern E-Commerce application.

---

## Business Objectives

- Enable online product discovery
- Improve customer shopping experience
- Support multiple payment methods
- Reduce order processing time
- Provide real-time order tracking
- Increase customer satisfaction
